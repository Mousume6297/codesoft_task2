About Karo Pitch

Karo Pitch is an initiative by KaroStartup created to help early-stage founders connect with investors and grow their businesses. Across India, thousands of founders are building innovative startups but struggle to access funding and mentorship. Karo Pitch bridges this gap by providing a platform where founders can pitch their ideas directly to investors.

How It Works

Apply

Submit your startup details and pitch deck.

Shortlisting

KaroStartup reviews applications and selects promising startups.

Pitch

Selected founders pitch their startups live to investors.

Funding

Investors discover and fund promising startups.
